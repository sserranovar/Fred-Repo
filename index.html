Frederick was born in Maryland in 1818 as a slave. His mom lived at a different plantation, so he never got to be with her, and the identity of his father is unknown.


Frederick Douglass died rom a heart attack in 1895 at age 77 when he was preparing to give a speech at a local church. Despite this, he had managed to bring the nation much closer to equality among races and the affects of his works are still felt today
